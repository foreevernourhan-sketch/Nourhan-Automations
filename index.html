FAQ

Do I need a new phone number?

Not always. You can use a new tracking number, or forward calls from your existing number depending on your setup.

What if SMS approval is pending?

You can still demo the workflows and call handling, and use internal notifications. Once SMS is approved, we enable live texting.

Will it sound like a bot?

No, we write messages in your voice, add natural delays, and keep replies short and human. The AI only answers from your provided content.

Can I turn the AI voice agent off?

Yes. The voice agent is optional. Many businesses start with missed-call textback and add voice later.
